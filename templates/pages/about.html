ABOUT THE UFC

The world's premier mixed martial arts organization since 1993

Our Story

The Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) is the world's premier mixed martial arts organization and the largest Pay-Per-View event provider in the world. UFC follows a rich history and tradition of competitive MMA dating back to the Olympic Games in Athens. About 2,000 years ago, the Greeks introduced the combat sport of Pankration, a fierce competition combining the striking skills of boxing with the grappling techniques of wrestling.

The UFC was founded in 1993 as a professional mixed martial arts organization that has revolutionized the fight business. With more than 700 million fans worldwide, UFC has produced over 500 events in 28 countries since its inception. UFC boasts the highest-level fighters on the planet and has popularized the sport globally through its powerful brand, world-class events, and unmatched programming.

In 2016, UFC was acquired by global sports, entertainment, and fashion leader Endeavor, along with strategic investors Silver Lake Partners and KKR, in what is widely recognized as one of the largest acquisitions in sports history.

The Evolution of UFC

When the UFC began in 1993, it was designed as a single-night tournament to determine the most effective martial art for unarmed combat situations. It featured minimal rules and fighters competing in different disciplines. Since then, the UFC has embraced numerous rule changes to create a more controlled and safer environment for its athletes while maintaining the integrity of competition.

Today, the UFC sets the gold standard for MMA events worldwide with a comprehensive set of unified rules and rigorous health and safety standards. The organization continues to evolve, constantly improving the sport and expanding its global footprint.

UFC Performance Institute

The UFC Performance Institute represents the world's first mixed martial arts multi-disciplinary research, innovation, and performance center. With locations in Las Vegas and Shanghai, these state-of-the-art facilities provide UFC athletes with unparalleled training, recovery, nutrition, and performance services.

The Performance Institute employs an expert staff of sports scientists, nutritionists, and strength and conditioning coaches who work directly with athletes to maximize their potential and optimize their overall health and wellness. This investment in athlete development demonstrates the UFC's commitment to the advancement of MMA as a professional sport.
